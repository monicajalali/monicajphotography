About Monica Jalali

Monica is a passionate landscape and travel photographer whose work captures the beauty of exploration and fleeting moments. Monica is rarely without a camera in hand, documenting the world around.

Over the years, she has dedicated herself to refining both her technical skills and personal style, blending her love for adventure with her creative vision. Her photography reflects a deep connection to nature, travel, and the stories found along the way. Each image serving as a timeless reminder of a place, a feeling, or a moment in motion.

Monica's journey through photography has continually pushed her mental and physical boundaries. She approaches her craft with a commitment to growth and a desire to inspire others to step outside their comfort zones and explore the world with fresh eyes.

In addition to her artistic pursuits, Monica has a background in healthcare, having worked for over nine years as a nurse in acute care. This experience has shaped her into a thoughtful, detail-oriented creator who brings a unique perspective to her work.

Monica invites you to follow along as she continues to evolve as an artist and adventurer.
